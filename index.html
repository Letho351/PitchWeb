Examinations

Here is where all your hard work pays off. Exams can be stressful, but proper preparation can make the process smoother and less daunting. UNISA offers a variety of online tools and resources to guide you through your examination journey.

Use the links on the left to navigate through important examination information, including dates, timetables, fees, and results. The "Examination results" section will display your latest marks once they are officially released.

Please note that all examination results are provisional until confirmed by the official academic record. If you believe there is an error in your results, you may apply for re-marking within the allowed period.
